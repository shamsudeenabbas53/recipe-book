Jollof Rice

Ingredients

IngredientQuantity
Rice3 cups
Tomato paste2 spoons
Oil1/2 cup
Onion1 large

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 40 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Steps

  1. Wash and parboil rice.
  2. Prepare tomato sauce with onion and oil.
  3. Add rice and cook until soft.

Creamy Pasta

Ingredients

IngredientQuantity
Pasta2 cups
Milk1 cup
Cheese1/2 cup
Butter2 spoons

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 2

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until soft.
  2. Prepare creamy sauce with butter and milk.
  3. Mix pasta with sauce and serve.

Source:https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fettuccine_Alfredo

Pancakes

Ingredients

IngredientQuantity
Flour2 cups
Milk1 cup
Eggs2
Sugar2 spoons

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 3

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Pour batter into a hot pan.
  3. Flip when golden brown and serve.

Source:https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pancake
